Celebrating the essence of Christmas and ending the year with joy!




We, the RNHS family, gathered together to embrace the spirit of Christmas. Our hearts overflowed with happiness as we witnessed our talented students enchanting the audience with their captivating performances. The SINGING DUO, melodious CHRISTMAS CAROL and the DANCE IDOL.

Amidst the glitz and glamour, one of the true highlights of this program was our "ADOPT A STUDENT" initiative. With immense pride and joy, we successfully provided Noche Buena packages and white RNHS T-shirts to those in need. This act of kindness truly embodies the essence of the season—spreading love, compassion, and solidarity.

Our SSLG officers, who went above and beyond to make this event even more special. They organized a heartwarming "Give a Gift" raffle for our beloved students, bringing smiles and excitement to everyone's faces.

Sa bawat ngiti, ngiti ng mga bata, at awit ng kaligayahan, nararamdaman natin ang tunay na diwa ng Pasko. Pagkakaisa sa gitna ng pagkakaiba.

Wishing you all a Merry Christmas and a Happy New Year filled with love, peace, and success RNHSians!
