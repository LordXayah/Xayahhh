Science Technology Engineering Mathematics

The Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics Strand (STEM) in the Philippines is a specialized educational track that focuses on preparing students for future careers in these fields. It aims to equip students with the necessary skills, knowledge, and experience to excel in science and technology-related fields.
